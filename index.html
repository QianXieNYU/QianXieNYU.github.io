About me

I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Civil and Urban Engineering at New York University Tandon School of Engineering, where I am advised by Professor Li Jin. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree in computer science and technology from Yao Class (Special Pilot Class of Computer Science founded by Turing Award Laureate, Prof. Andrew Chi-Chih Yao), Institute of Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University. I have a broad interest in operations research, applied probability, and data science. Currently, my research focuses on the application of queuing theory, game theory and stochastic model to control, optimization and learning of network systems. Applications include intelligent transportation system and cyber-physical system.
