About me

I am a second-year PhD student at New York University Tandon School of Engineering. Prior to that, I received bachelor's degree in computer science and technology from Yao Class, Institute of Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University. I will transfer to Cornell ORIE this fall. I have a broad interest in operations research, applied probability, and data science. Currently, my research focuses on using queuing theory, game theory and stochastic model to control, optimization and learning of network systems. Applications include intelligent transportation system and cyber-physical system.
