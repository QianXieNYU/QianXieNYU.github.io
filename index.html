About me

I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Civil and Urban Engineering at New York University Tandon School of Engineering, where I am advised by Professor Li Jin. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree in computer science and technology from Yao Class at Tsinghua University. My research interests lie in the application of queuing theory, game theory and stochastic model to control, optimization and learning of network systems, with particular focus on intelligent transportation systems and cyber-physical systems. I'm also interested in operations research and data-driven modeling.
